Cannabinoids & Terpenes

What are Cannabinoids and Terpenes?

Cannabis contains an array of compounds, namely, cannabinoids and terpenes. Cannabinoids can either be psychoactive (like THC) or nonpsychoactive (like CBD). Each cannabinoid exerts different effects on the body. Terpenes, on the other hand, are found all throughout food and plant life. They affect the scent and flavor of cannabis, and have their own set of therapeutic properties. Although cannabinoids and terpenes are distinct bioactive compounds, it is widely believed the combination of cannabinoids and terpenes together affects the experience of each cannabis stain and product. This is known as the entourage effect.
